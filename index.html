Indulge in the creamy, coffee-infused layers of Tiramisu.

About Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a traditional Italian dessert made with layers of mascarpone cheese, coffee-soaked ladyfingers, and a dusting of cocoa powder. Its name means "pick-me-up" in Italian, reflecting the energizing coffee in its recipe.

Recipe

  • 1 cup of strong coffee
  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 package of ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Prepare by layering coffee-soaked ladyfingers with mascarpone cream, then dust with cocoa powder. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
